About FARM SATHI

Our project aims to revolutionize farming by providing farmers with a user- friendly mobile application that offers essential information, market insights, and a community platform. This tool will address the challenges faced by farmers, especially in rural areas.

Farmers, especially in rural areas, face numerous challenges including limited access to crucial information, fluctuating market prices, and a lack of modern agricultural techniques. This project aims to address these issues by providing a comprehensive digital platform tailored to the needs of farmers.

This app will employ modern technology to deliver weather forecasts, crop-specific advice, market insights, Government Schemes and a platform for community interaction. We will utilize agile development methodologies for efficient and iterative progress.
